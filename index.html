NAME: La casa de los famosos

Sinopsis: La Casa de los Famosos is a reality TV show where a group of celebrities live together in a house, isolated from the outside world. The participants are constantly monitored by cameras and microphones, capturing their interactions, alliances, and conflicts. Each week, housemates nominate each other for elimination, and the public votes to evict one of them. The last remaining celebrity wins a cash prize. The show is known for its drama, strategic gameplay, and the mix of personalities from various entertainment fields.

NAME: Invencible

Sinopsis: Invincible is an animated superhero TV series based on the comic book of the same name by Robert Kirkman. The show follows Mark Grayson, a teenager who seems like a normal high school student, except that his father is Omni-Man, the most powerful superhero on Earth. As Mark turns 17, he begins to develop his own superpowers and takes on the superhero identity "Invincible." However, as he learns to navigate his new abilities and responsibilities, he uncovers dark secrets about his father and the true nature of his family.

NAME: Un show mas

Sinopsis: Regular Show is an animated television series created by J.G. Quintel that aired on Cartoon Network from 2010 to 2017. The show follows the lives of two best friends, Mordecai, a blue jay, and Rigby, a raccoon, who work as groundskeepers at a park. Despite their mundane job, the duo constantly finds themselves in surreal and often absurd situations that involve supernatural elements, time travel, and bizarre creatures. Their boss, Benson (a gumball machine), and co-workers, including Skips (a yeti), Pops (a lollipop-shaped man), Muscle Man, and Hi-Five Ghost, also play significant roles in their misadventures.

NAME: Lucifer

Sinopsis: Lucifer is a popular TV series that originally premiered on Fox before being picked up by Netflix. The show is based on the DC Comics character created by Neil Gaiman for the Sandman comic series, later featured in a spin-off comic by Mike Carey. In the series, Lucifer Morningstar, portrayed by Tom Ellis, is the Devil who has grown bored with ruling Hell. He decides to abandon his throne and move to Los Angeles, where he opens a nightclub called Lux and begins to enjoy a more hedonistic life. However, his life takes a turn when he becomes involved in solving crimes with LAPD detective Chloe Decker. Over time, Lucifer uses his powers of persuasion and immortality to assist in investigations, all while dealing with supernatural threats, internal struggles with his identity, and his complicated relationship with Heaven and Hell.

NAME: Dark

Sinopsis: Dark is a German sci-fi thriller series that premiered on Netflix in 2017. Created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese, it is a complex and mind-bending story that revolves around the mysterious disappearance of children in the small town of Winden. The show explores the hidden connections between four estranged families, revealing a deep-rooted mystery involving time travel, parallel universes, and a cycle that spans several generations.
